Kids were playing with the dog

By Maeve

The dogs were very happy, they ran through grass. And they lived on a farm. They liked to play in their doghouse. They explored a little bit. Then they got lost. They ran around barking and some people noticed them. The owners came and found them.

What to do if you find a lost dog

  1. Bring it home
  2. Help it
  3. Find its owner
